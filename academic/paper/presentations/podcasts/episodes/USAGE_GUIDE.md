## Quick Start with NotebookLM (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Upload First Episode
1. Go to https://notebooklm.google.com
2. Click "New Notebook"
3. Upload `pdf/E001_project_overview_and_introduction.pdf`

### Step 2: Generate Podcast
1. Click "Notebook guide" (bottom right)
2. Click "Generate" under "Audio Overview"
3. Wait 3-5 minutes for processing

### Step 3: Download
1. Click play button to preview
2. Click download icon to save MP3
3. File will be ~15-20 minutes of audio

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Upload in batches:** NotebookLM handles 5-10 sources per notebook well
2. **Process during off-peak:** Faster generation times in evening/morning
3. **Download immediately:** Generated audio expires after 30 days
4. **Organize files:** Create folders for each part (foundations, infrastructure, etc.)
5. **Use markdown for missing PDFs:** Copy text to Google Doc format

## Troubleshooting

**Q: NotebookLM says "too many sources"**
A: Limit to 10 PDFs per notebook, create multiple notebooks

**Q: Can I use markdown files?**
A: Yes! Copy content to Google Doc and upload

**Q: Audio generation failed**
A: Try again during off-peak hours, or split into smaller batches

**Q: Can I customize the audio?**
A: NotebookLM generates automatically, no customization options yet
