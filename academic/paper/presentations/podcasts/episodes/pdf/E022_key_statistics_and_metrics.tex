\section{Memory Leak Prevention}

**Long-Duration Simulation Test:**

    **Test Scenario:**
    
        - 10,000 simulations sequentially
        - Monitor memory growth
        - Target: <10\

    **Results:**
    
        - **Initial memory:** 85 MB
        - **Final memory:** 92 MB (+8.2\
        - **Peak memory:** 105 MB (during PSO)
        - **Verdict:** [OK] No significant leaks

    **Prevention Mechanisms:**
    
        - Explicit `cleanup()` calls after simulations
        - Bounded history buffers (FIFO)
        - Periodic `gc.collect()` in batch simulations
        - Weakref for callback references
